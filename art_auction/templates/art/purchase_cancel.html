ARTIV PURCHASE Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: 07.02.2025 | Effective: 01.03.2025

General Policy

In addition to auctions, ARTIV offers direct purchases, where buyers can purchase artworks instantly. To ensure a smooth experience, the following cancellation rules apply.

Buyer Cancellations

  • Orders cannot be cancelled once the artwork is shipped.
  • If a buyer wishes to cancel an order, they must request it within 1 hour of purchase.
  • If an order is already shipped, cancellations are not possible.

Seller Cancellations

  • Sellers must fulfill orders once a purchase is confirmed.
  • If a seller is unable to fulfill an order (e.g., artwork is damaged, unavailable), ARTIV will issue a full refund to the buyer.

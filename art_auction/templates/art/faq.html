Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

"ARTIV" is a platform that will give some emerging Artists who would otherwise not be able to share their work online the chance to do so. Auctions are popular because both buyers and sellers believe they will get a pretty good deal buying or selling assets.

To place a bid, click on the Details button of the product you like, enter the bid amount, and then click on "Bid Now" button.
Note: You must be logged in to the website to place a bid.

If a Seller uploads an artwork and if it is not sold till the time of auction, then it will directly go to the "Unsold Items" section. From there, you can still bid on that artwork and buy it.

If someone wants to sell the artworks, then he/she can Register as a Seller in "Become a Seller" section.

"ARTIV" provides you with the feature of AR technology (Augmented Reality), where you can see how the Artwork will look at your home.

There is only 1 quantity of artwork because the artworks here are NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens). The Seller has only 1 copy of the Artwork.
